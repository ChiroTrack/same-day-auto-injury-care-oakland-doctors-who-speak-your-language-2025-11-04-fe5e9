About Oakland Accident Care

Founded in 2018, Oakland Accident Care was established by a group of compassionate physicians who witnessed firsthand the struggles accident victims face when navigating the complex intersection of medical care, insurance claims, and legal proceedings. Our founders recognized that most patients weren't receiving the specialized, trauma-informed care they deserved during their most vulnerable moments. We built our practice on the principle that healing should be accessible, immediate, and free from financial barriers—no matter your insurance status or pending legal case.

Today, we remain committed to our core mission: providing same-day, trauma-sensitive care to every accident victim who walks through our doors. We believe that your recovery matters more than paperwork, your health comes before insurance approval, and you deserve a medical team that truly understands what you're going through. Our multidisciplinary approach combines evidence-based medicine with genuine compassion, ensuring that each patient receives personalized treatment that addresses their unique physical, emotional, and legal needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

We understand that time is critical after an auto accident. We offer same-day appointments for accident victims whenever possible. In most cases, you can be seen within 24 hours of your call. Our scheduling team works with you to find the fastest available time slot that fits your needs. Simply call us or use our online booking system to secure your appointment immediately.

Yes, we accept all major insurance plans including auto insurance, health insurance, workers' compensation, and liens from personal injury attorneys. We also work with uninsured and underinsured patients. Our billing department handles all insurance communications and claims processing, so you don't have to worry about the details. We never turn away a patient due to insurance issues—your health is our priority.

Financial concerns should never prevent you from getting the care you need. We accept liens from personal injury attorneys, meaning your attorney can guarantee payment from your settlement. We also work with patients on flexible payment plans. If you have a pending legal case, we can bill directly to your attorney's lien. Our goal is to remove all financial barriers to your recovery, so please discuss your situation with our front desk team.

Our comprehensive treatment approach includes medical evaluation and diagnosis, pain management, physical therapy and rehabilitation, chiropractic care, trauma counseling, and coordination with specialists as needed. We create personalized treatment plans based on your specific injuries and needs. Our team may recommend a combination of treatments to address both immediate pain and long-term recovery. We use evidence-based techniques and the latest medical technology to ensure optimal outcomes.

We have extensive experience working with personal injury attorneys and insurance companies. Our medical team provides detailed documentation of your injuries and treatment progress that supports your legal case. We communicate directly with your attorney and insurance company as needed, providing expert opinions and medical reports. We understand the legal timeline and requirements, ensuring our documentation strengthens your claim. Your attorney can contact us directly with any questions or requests for medical records.
